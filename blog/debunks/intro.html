Debunks

I know most my audiance(and me), are tech nerds, so, to generalise, we love the objective, and loathe dealing with what's subjective. The property of being subjective, is something frauds1 Frauds in this sense can mean fraudulent ideas as well as people are experts at harnessing. That's what the art of debunking is all about, turning subjectively right, into objectivly wrong, and me, your hero, is here to do that.

Only if it were that simple…

By aruging with them, you're only helping make their lie better

Flint Dibble (a great archaeological debunker)

It would be that simple if the frauds did not have a fast and loose relationship with the truth, but sadly that is not the case. Those who have been conviced have lied to themselves, will not hesitate to do it again.

In this sense, a fraud is a hydra, you argue with the idea he'll just have more. For a full debunk to take place, you must objectively show that the idea is not only wrong, but has evil roots. In this sense, you cannot look to the person who believes crap as an unaware good intended simpleton.

In nerd culture in general, we mostly never ever resort to Ad hominem, but in debunks, ad hominem is necessary.
